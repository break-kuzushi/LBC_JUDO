About Our Judo School

Welcome to Long Beach Judo, a premier destination for world-class martial arts instruction inspired by the renowned Jigoro Kano. Our school is founded on the principles of discipline, strength, and mastery, with a focus on the art of Judo. Our team of internationally recognized instructors brings their wealth of experience and knowledge to provide the highest quality training in Judo.

At Long Beach Judo, we welcome individuals of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced martial artists. Our mission is to guide you on your journey to success, whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or aiming to refine your skills. We believe in the power of martial arts to transform lives, instill confidence, and foster a sense of community. Join us at Long Beach Jodo and experience the difference of training with the best.
